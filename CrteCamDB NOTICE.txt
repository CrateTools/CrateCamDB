## How to credit CrateCamDB

When using this database, include a credit line in your tool's About page, documentation, or `_metadata` block. Suggested wording:

> Camera reference data from CrateCamDB ([link to your CrateCamDB location]) — licensed under CC-BY-4.0.

If you've modified entries, please indicate that you've done so in your credit. For example:

> Camera reference data based on CrateCamDB ([link]), with modifications. Licensed under CC-BY-4.0.

## Upstream attribution

This database is derived in part from **[VFXCamDB.com](https://vfxcamdb.com)**, maintained by **Tony D'Agostino** since 2010, and is used with his permission.

VFXCamDB is the foundational reference for digital cinema sensor specifications in the VFX community, and CrateCamDB would not exist in its current form without it. We strongly encourage downstream users — whether you're integrating this data into a tool, publishing derivative works, or simply distributing copies — to preserve credit to VFXCamDB alongside your CrateCamDB attribution.

A combined attribution might read:

> Camera reference data from CrateCamDB ([link]), licensed under CC-BY-4.0. Includes data derived from VFXCamDB.com (Tony D'Agostino), used with permission.

## Disclaimer

Camera specifications in this database are provided as-is for reference. While care has been taken to verify accuracy against manufacturer documentation, errors are possible. For production-critical applications (matchmove, virtual production, etc.), users are advised to verify sensor dimensions against the camera manufacturer's official specifications.
